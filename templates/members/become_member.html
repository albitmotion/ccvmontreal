Become a Member



Why join the CCV?


Joining an association is more than just signing up—it’s becoming part of a community that shares your values, goals, and passion. As a member, you gain access to valuable resources, exclusive events, and opportunities to network with like-minded individuals. Membership provides a platform to learn, grow, and stay informed about the latest trends in your field. You’ll also have a voice in shaping initiatives and projects that make a real difference. Together, we can achieve more—become a member today and help us build a stronger, connected future.



How to become a member?


Joining our association is simple and rewarding! Follow these easy steps:

  • Fill Out the Membership Form – Complete the online application with your details.
  • Choose Your Membership Type – Select the plan that best suits your needs.
  • Submit Your Payment – Pay securely online or via the accepted methods listed.
  • Confirmation – You’ll receive a welcome email with all your member benefits.

Start your journey today and enjoy the advantages of being part of a strong, connected community!
